Ingredients of AP's Speciality Omelete:

  • One or two conventional eggs
  • Peppers
  • Seasoning spices preferably chili or cayenee powders
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spring onion
  • Optionally additional cooked meats or condiments on side or toppings

Cooking Process:

    1. Prepare and clean your cooking space ("Mise en place")

    2. Crack one or two eggs then wisk it until its fully liquated

    3. Add 5mL (Tea Spoon) or more of peppers

    4. Add 3mL (Half Tea Spoon) of spice of your choice

    5. Cut into milimeter slices of Onions as seasonings

    6. Cut into milimeter slices of Spring onions as seasonings

    7. Mix it all then slow cook it (50째C - 70째C or 122째F - 158째F) and continue mixing for 6 minutes
